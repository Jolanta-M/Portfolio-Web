The gap in existing mobile decision applications

After reviewing psychological literature on the topic of human decision making process and the existing mobile decision applications I discovered that most apps are providing only the analytical decision tools such as Pros & Cons, Eisenhower Matrix etc., but almost none are providing the tools to master the intuitive decision style, otherwise known as ‘gut feeling’.
